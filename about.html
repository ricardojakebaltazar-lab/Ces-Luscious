About Ces Luscious Foods

✨ "Premium Taste, Seamless Service, Affordable Joy." — Since 2019

📖 Our Story ✏️ Edit

Ces Luscious Foods was established in 2019 under the management of Cecile Muncal. What started as a small home-based business has grown into a beloved brand known for delivering "sweetness for every occasion" through authentic Filipino delicacies and sweet desserts.

Our journey began with a passion for creating traditional Filipino treats that bring joy to every celebration. From humble beginnings, we've expanded our reach through social media, connecting with customers who share our love for authentic, high-quality desserts.

Today, Ces Luscious Foods is proud to offer a wide range of products including puto, leche flan, cakes, palitaw, mango graham, ube halaya, and maja blanca — each made with love and the finest ingredients.

🎯 Our Mission ✏️ Edit

To provide premium taste, seamless service, and affordable joy to every customer, while preserving the authentic flavors of Filipino desserts. We are committed to crafting each product with integrity, using quality ingredients that honor traditional recipes and bring families together.

We aim to create delightful experiences — from the first bite to the final moment of satisfaction. Through continuous improvement and heartfelt service, we ensure that every dessert tells a story of passion, heritage, and genuine sweetness. Our mission is to be a cherished part of your celebrations, big or small, and share the warmth of Filipino delicacies with every home.
